This page is dedicated to the main characters in game







The two main characters in Cyberpunk 2077 are V, that is the customizable protagonist controlled by the player, and Johnny Silverhand, a Rock 'n' Roll star turned Semi-Terrorist towards Arasaka Corporation.

For the first trailers released they used the male version of V to promote their game, and then when they decided to release a DLC they wanted to start anew, so they changed to the Female V for the DLC trailers to breathe new life into the most controversial release of a AAA game in recent times.

I will not go to deep into any lore as this will not be my finished website and I plan to include more as I finish the game.
